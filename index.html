About Our Institute

A legacy of excellence, committed to shaping future generations.

A Tradition of Educational Excellence

Established with a vision to provide quality education accessible to all, D.H. Schooling System has grown into a premier educational institution. We combine traditional values with modern pedagogical techniques.

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, providing an ideal environment for students to discover their potential across school, college, and specialized coaching programs.

Our Mission

To impart quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values required to thrive in a dynamic world.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, holistic development, and producing socially responsible citizens.

Our Values

Integrity, Respect, Excellence, Compassion, and a commitment to lifelong learning form the core of our institutional ethos.

Our Journey

2005 - The Beginning

Founded as a small primary school with just 50 students.

2012 - Expansion

Upgraded to Matriculation level, moving to a larger custom-built campus.

2018 - College & Coaching

Introduced Intermediate classes and specialized evening coaching centers.

Today - Complete Education Hub

Integrating School, College, Coaching, and Madarasa studies under one roof.

Academic Programs

Discover our diverse educational pathways.

School Program

From Pre-Primary to Matriculation, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to build a strong academic foundation. Our focus is on interactive learning, critical thinking, and character building.

  • Early Childhood Education (Montessori based)
  • Primary Section (Grades 1-5)
  • Secondary Section (Grades 6-8)
  • Matriculation (Science & Computer Groups)

College Program

Our college section prepares students for higher education challenges. We provide specialized faculties, well-equipped laboratories, and career counseling to guide students toward their professional goals.

  • Pre-Medical
  • Pre-Engineering
  • Computer Science (ICS)
  • Commerce (I.Com)

Coaching Classes

Our evening coaching center is designed for students seeking extra academic support or aiming for top board positions. We offer rigorous testing sessions and expert faculty guidance.

  • Subject-specific expert teachers
  • Regular mock exams and assessments
  • Past paper solving sessions
  • Small batch sizes for personal attention

Madarasa Education

Unique to our system is the seamless integration of Islamic studies. We provide a structured Madarasa curriculum alongside contemporary education to nurture students spiritually and academically.

  • Nazra Quran with Tajweed
  • Hifz-ul-Quran program
  • Basic Islamic Fiqh & Seerah
  • Tarbiyah and moral grooming
